# ACL Next Large Goal

Date: 2026-05-26.

## Summary

The next large goal for the ACL/ARR paper is now recorded as:

> Lock the ACL-family submission route and complete the final integrity/upload
> gate for the current ACL/ARR candidate packet.

This is codified in `paper/venues/acl27/NEXT_LARGE_GOAL.md`.

## Decision

The next goal should not be another open-ended experiment sweep. The current
candidate already has a VLM grounding story, scoped InternNav official-scene
sanity evidence, material-effect baseline boundaries, OpenReview checklist
material, metadata material, and a minimal safe staging script.

The remaining submission risk is route/form/integrity/upload readiness:

- choose EACL 2027 via ARR now, or wait for Annual ACL 2027 official policy;
- refresh the selected venue policy immediately before upload;
- record author-only OpenReview fields;
- rebuild, restage, and anonymization-scan the exact packet;
- keep optional videos out unless separately approved.

## InternNav And Video Scope

The next goal uses official InternNav / KuJiaLe evidence as downstream sanity
evidence. GRScenes remains the VLM grounding stress-set evidence; it should not
be inflated into a broad navigation benchmark.

Selected qualitative videos remain optional. They are useful for figures and
reviewer response, but they are qualitative inspection evidence and are excluded
from the default safe upload packet.
