# ACL Target Call Policy Audit

Date: 2026-05-26

## Scope

This pass moved the submission rehearsal from a vague "ACL/ARR target-call
check" to an explicit venue decision record. It did not change the manuscript's
scientific claims or add experiments.

## Findings

- Annual ACL 2027 remains unavailable as a public official target in the
  checked official sources: no Annual ACL 2027 CFP, author kit, city/date page,
  commitment deadline, or conference-specific supplement policy was found.
- EACL 2027 is now a concrete public 2027 ACL-family route: the official site is
  live, names Athens, Greece and March 9-14, 2027, and lists August 3, 2026 as
  the ARR submission deadline.
- ARR Dates and Venues also lists EACL 2027 with final ARR submission date
  August 3, 2026 and commitment date October 11, 2026.
- The EACL 2027 main-paper call page still says the full CFP and detailed
  timetable are being finalized, so this is not a complete venue-specific
  final-upload lock yet.

## Practical Decision

The paper is ACL/ARR candidate-ready. If the authors want a concrete public
2027 ACL-family target now, the policy-backed route is EACL 2027 via ARR. If the
authors specifically want Annual ACL 2027, the repo should remain in candidate
mode until the official Annual ACL 2027 call appears.
