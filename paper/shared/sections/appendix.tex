Table~\ref{tab:vlm_coordinate_baselines} adds deterministic coordinate-only
baselines for the expanded30 stress set. These rows are not model predictions.
They show that raw pixel scoring is easy to saturate under target-centered
camera construction: both the image-center baseline and the pixel bbox-center
oracle score 30/30 under raw point-in-box. The normalized-1000 bbox-center
oracle is the corresponding upper bound for the prompt contract used by the
Gemma4 stress run.
