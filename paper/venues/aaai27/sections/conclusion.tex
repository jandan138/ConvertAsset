\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

Across the GRScenes, selected NVIDIA, and official InternNav checks, the
supported claim is not broad robustness or speedup; it is a reproducible way to
ask whether material conversion changes language-grounding evidence. The
practical outcome is an evidence gate: converted-asset benchmarks should expose
matched renders, projection QA, prompt and coordinate contracts,
material-risk bins, and embodied smoke gates before promoting converted
synthetic scenes into downstream reliability claims.
