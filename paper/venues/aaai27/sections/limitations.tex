\section*{Limitations}

The evidence base is intentionally narrow. The clean visual-QA pool has 15
pairs, below the configured 20-pair clean final gate, so it is not evidence that
conversion preserves VLM grounding in general. The 30-pair expanded stress set is
a frozen, target-centered material-shift set with 4 PASS and 26 WARN pairs; it
supports a controlled stress-test claim, not a broad GRScenes benchmark
distribution. Qwen2.5-VL also exposes unresolved coordinate semantics because
raw image-space scoring is stronger than the requested normalized-1000
interpretation.

The material-effect and embodied checks are similarly claim-bounded. The current
matrix covers 30 GRScenes samples for four effect bins plus clearcoat and
procedural fixtures; it supports selected qualitative and failure-case
interpretation, not a material-effect error-rate distribution. The
InternNav/DualVLN~\cite{Wei2026Ground} route is an official-route sanity check
over three local KuJiaLe \texttt{val\_unseen} scenes and 99 paired episodes, not
evidence for all InternNav or InteriorAgent settings, R2R/MP3D, manipulation,
or broad GRScenes robustness. Figure~\ref{fig:internnav-rollout-supp} is
qualitative orientation evidence only. The official-scene load/render closure
supports stability only, not speedup.
