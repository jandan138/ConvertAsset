Current experiment state on 2026-05-22: source selection, target localization, paired render planning, full scratch no-MDL conversion, render-smoke execution, projection QA, blind visual QA, and the first real-model VLM probe are implemented. `source_manifest.json` fixes 5 episode-backed home scenes, 5 commercial stress scenes, and 40 selected episode records. `target_manifest.json` resolves all 40 selected episode-backed records to USD prim paths and world-space bboxes using original GRScenes scene USDs read-only; these records collapse to 23 unique spatial targets because some `mm` and `sn` episodes refer to the same object. `render_manifest.json` plans 4 target-centered views per unique target, giving 92 original/converted view pairs and 184 material-condition render jobs. The scratch no-MDL route has converted 99 top-level raw scenes and verification records `passed=true`, while the original `/cpfs/user/zhuzihou/assets/zzh-grscenes` source tree remains free of `_noMDL` sidecar pollution.

GRScenes VLM evidence is now beyond plumbing but still below final paper scale. The first 10 recommended render pairs plus 11 additional centerline-clear alternatives have passed render-smoke and projection QA. Independent blind visual QA leaves a clean pool of 4 PASS original/converted pairs: one bottle, two cup views, and one faucet view. The strongest current positive real-model artifact is the local Gemma4 PASS-only probe under `../../shared/evidence/raw/grscene_vlm_grounding/probes/gemma4_pass_only_*`: 8 predictions over 4 PASS pairs, answer accuracy 4/4 for both original and converted, normalized-1000 point-in-bbox 4/4 original and 3/4 converted, normalized-1000 pair hit agreement 3/4, and mean normalized point delta 27.047455 px. The second-backend Qwen2.5-VL probe under `../../shared/evidence/raw/grscene_vlm_grounding/probes/qwen25_pass_only_*` is protocol-sensitivity evidence: direct JSON prompting produced zero parsed answers or points, while structured-text prompting produced 8 parsed rows, answer accuracy 3/4 for both material conditions under the current strict expected-label matcher, raw point-in-bbox 2/3 original and 2/4 converted among parsed points, and normalized-1000 point-in-bbox 0/3 original and 0/4 converted. The Qwen coordinate semantics remain unresolved because raw image-space boxes score better than normalized scaling. This is pilot evidence only, not canonical VLM performance: the ACL version still needs a frozen coordinate/response-format protocol, a larger visual-QA PASS set or retakes, canonical `predictions.jsonl` / `score_summary.json`, downstream navigation evidence if kept, and paper figures/tables derived from those final artifacts.
