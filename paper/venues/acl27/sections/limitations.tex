\section*{Limitations}

This evaluation is limited by design and scale. The clean visual-QA pool has 15
pairs, below the configured 20-pair clean final set, so it supports a pilot
check rather than broad VLM grounding conclusions. The 30-pair expanded stress set is frozen and
target-centered: it contains 4 PASS and 26 WARN pairs, and supports a controlled
material-shift stress-test result rather than a broad benchmark over GRScenes.
\mbox{Qwen2.5-VL} also leaves coordinate use open: raw image-space and
normalized-1000 scores disagree.

Material and stack checks follow the same evaluation scope here. The audit covers 30
GRScenes samples across four effect bins; clearcoat and texture fixtures are
separate cases. It covers selected cases rather than a rate sample. The
cited route~\cite{Wei2026Ground} is a stack-entry check over three KuJiaLe
\texttt{val\_unseen} scenes and 99 paired episodes. InternNav variants,
\mbox{InteriorAgent}, R2R/MP3D, \mbox{manipulation}, and broader GRScenes
robustness remain outside this limited tested setting. The rollout panel only orients readers. Load/render
measures stability only, not speedup.
