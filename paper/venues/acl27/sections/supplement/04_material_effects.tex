\section{Material-Effect and NVIDIA Diagnostics}
\label{sec:supp-material-effects}

The material-effect audit compares original MDL, ConvertAsset noMDL, and the
NVIDIA PreviewSurface baseline only inside bins with selected evidence. The
purpose is to make material risk visible before converted assets are used in
VLM or embodied-language evaluation; global converter ranking would need a
broader audit.
