clean-pool table in the main paper and is retained here as a provenance bridge:
it shows how the protocol moved from a small visually safe set to the larger
clean pool and then to the frozen material-shift stress set.

The zoom-stress views differ from clean preservation examples. They make the
target visible under material, color, or lighting shifts so that point behavior
can be inspected; material-mechanism coverage remains limited to the audited
cases.
