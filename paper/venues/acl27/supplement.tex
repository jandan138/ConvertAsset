\section*{Supplement Overview}
This supplement expands the evidence and diagnostic material behind the main
paper. The main submission remains self-contained: no main-paper result depends
on reviewers reading this document. The supplement provides derivations,
additional tables, selected visual panels, selected InternNav stills, media
inventory notes, and hypothesis-level interpretation for readers who want to
audit the evidence chain in more detail.

The diagnostics are positioned against synthetic embodied-AI scene resources,
grounding protocols, navigation metrics, and vision-model similarity checks
used in the main paper's evidence chain
\citep{Wang2024GRUtopia,Wei2026Ground,Anderson2018Vision,Wang2004Image,Radford2021Learning,Oquab2023DINOv2}.

All selected visual and navigation examples are qualitative evidence. They do
not replace the frozen VLM tables, the material-effect risk matrix, or the
99-episode InternNav paired run used by the main paper.
